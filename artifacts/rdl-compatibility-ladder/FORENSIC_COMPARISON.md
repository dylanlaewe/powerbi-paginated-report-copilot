## Collection-count findings

The deterministic output in `COLLECTION_CONSISTENCY.json` verifies:

- dataset field order equals ElementPath order and every embedded row's element order;
- DesignerState row, column, and coordinate counts are complete and unique;
- field names and DataField sources are nonempty and unique;
- every tablix row spans exactly the declared body-column count;
- column-hierarchy leaf counts equal body-column counts;
- row-hierarchy leaf counts equal body-row counts;
- group names are unique;
- ElementPath types belong to Microsoft's documented grammar; and
- body width does not exceed printable page width.

Both the proven seed and rejected artifact pass these checks. Therefore the evidence does **not** support a collection-count mismatch as the root cause. The compatibility ladder must isolate the Report Builder-specific structural and serialization differences above.
